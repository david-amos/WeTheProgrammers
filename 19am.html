19th Amendment

The right of citizens of the United States to vote shall not be denied or abridged by the United States or by any State on account of sex.

Why do we have this amendment

This amendment was passed so that women could gain the right to voice their opinion and vote. before this women did not have much of a voice when it came to the united states governemnt. this was one of the first steps on the long road to equal rights for women.
